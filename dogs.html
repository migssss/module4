Max and Belle

For Harvey Days Gone Bye Beside the Dying Fire Max and Belle
about friends goshu ☰

I adopted 2 dogs about two and a half years ago. They are a hassle, yet they help make your day more blissfull. Belle is 7 years old, has broken her teeth in anger, ran away from us for hours because of fireworks, and has growled and threaten any animal that attempts to steal her attention from us. Just a great happy dog. And then we have Max. What many would consider the "special" one of the group, but what I would describe as the "dumb" one of the group. Max is 8 years old and fortunately for him, has never caused us any type of trouble other than sneaking bites away from our food when we look away. How else do you think he gained 10lbs behind our back over the years? love these two with my heart. And I will cherish and show them the happy life they deserved before.
